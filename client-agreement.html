Client Agreement

Last updated: 1 July 2024 · Tax Agent No. 26233096 · The Accounting Academy Pty Ltd
Plain English summary

By using our service, you agree to: give us accurate information, pay our fee when your return is lodged, and let us act as your registered tax agent with the ATO. We agree to prepare your return correctly, keep your information confidential, and be honest about what you will receive before you commit.

Client
You — the person using the service and entering into this agreement
Provider
Working Holiday Tax, under The Accounting Academy Pty Ltd
01

Accepting these terms

By using our service — whether by messaging us, providing your tax details, or asking us to prepare your return — you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this agreement. This agreement is between you and Working Holiday Tax, operating under The Accounting Academy Pty Ltd (ABN 26 669 927 959), Tax Agent No. 26233096.

02

Our fees

Standard fee for a tax return: $200 + GST. Complex returns are quoted before we start. Super (DASP) claims are charged as a percentage of the amount recovered, confirmed before we begin.

You pay only when your return is lodged — never upfront. Checking what you are entitled to is always free.
03

Your responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate, complete, and honest information. If anything changes after submission, notify us promptly.

Providing false information to the ATO is a serious offence. If incorrect information results in an incorrect return, you are responsible for any penalties or additional tax the ATO applies.
04

What we do as your tax agent

By using our service, you authorise us to: add your TFN to our ATO client list; access your ATO records; prepare and lodge your tax return; receive ATO correspondence; and apply for Medicare levy exemption certificates where applicable.

We always show you the completed return before lodging. Nothing goes to the ATO without your confirmation.
05

No refund guarantee

We prepare your return correctly based on information you provide and applicable tax law. We cannot guarantee a specific refund amount — the final outcome is determined by the ATO. Ask us for a free estimate before we start.

06

Confidentiality and privacy

Everything you share with us is treated as strictly confidential. We disclose information only to the ATO as required, to the TPB if legally required, or with your explicit consent. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

07

Termination

Either party can end this agreement at any time. If you terminate before we lodge, no fee is charged. If we have already lodged, the fee applies. We may terminate immediately if you provide false information or conduct is unacceptable.

08

Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of Victoria, Australia. Disputes should first be raised directly with us. If unresolved, they may be referred to mediation before any court proceedings.

Questions before you agree?

By using our service you accept these terms. If anything is not clear, message us before we start. No jargon, no pressure.
